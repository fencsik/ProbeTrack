    Thank you for volunteering to participate in this experiment. The
    purpose of this experiment is to investigate how you keep track of
    multiple moving targets during brief disruptions, and how distracting
    tasks affect your attention to such tasks.  The data you helped us
    collect will be used to answer basic questions about how our visual
    system works in situations like driving a car or piloting an airplane.
    The results may be incorporated into a conference presentation or a
    published research article.

    If you have any questions or concerns about this study, please contact
    Prof.\ David Fencsik by phone at 510-885-2384 or by email at
    david.fencsik@csueastbay.edu.
